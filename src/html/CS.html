Overview of Computer Science at National University of Management

The Bachelor of Computer Science program at the National University of Management provides students with a strong foundation in the principles of computing and software development. The program covers various aspects of computer science, including algorithms, programming languages, data structures, databases, and software engineering. Graduates are equipped to contribute to the ever-evolving tech industry, tackling real-world challenges in areas like AI, cybersecurity, and cloud computing.

Curriculum & Subjects

  • Introduction to Computer Science
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems
  • Object-Oriented Programming
  • Operating Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • Computer Networks
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Cloud Computing
  • Cybersecurity

Career Opportunities for Computer Science Graduates

Graduates of the Computer Science program can pursue a wide range of careers in various fields such as software development, data science, cybersecurity, and AI. Potential job roles include:

  • Software Developer
  • Web Developer
  • Data Scientist
  • Cybersecurity Analyst
  • Systems Analyst
  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • Cloud Solutions Architect

Why Choose Computer Science at National University of Management?

The Computer Science program at the National University of Management prepares students with both theoretical knowledge and practical experience in software development, algorithms, and emerging technologies. With our modern computer labs, collaborative projects, and industry partnerships, students are well-prepared for the demands of the tech industry.
